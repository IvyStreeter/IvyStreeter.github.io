4.1 Basics and Fundamentals

4.1.1 Introduction to C++

Cons:

  • Considered challengin to learn
  • Easy to instroduce but has terrible bugs (has no fallback to protect you from your own poor coding)
  • Often time-consuming, compiling is complicated on different platforms and computers. Whereas Java and Python do not have this issue.
  • Not truly platform-independent

Pros: - Fast - Low-level / hardware access - Can get a lot of processing done in a short time - Good for high-end games, AI, device drivers - Pushes you to understand your computer - Bade of honor
